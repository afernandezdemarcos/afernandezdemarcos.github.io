Research Interest

My research is focused on directional statistics, a field of statistics where data supports are different than the Euclidean. Specifically, I am interested in hypothesis testing problems with data lying on the hypersphere $$ \mathbb{S}^q:=\{\mathbf{x}\in \mathbb{R}^{q+1}:\|\mathbf{x}\|= 1\}, q\geq 1.$$ My PhD research, advised by Eduardo García-Portugués, aims to develop one- and $k$-samples tests of uniformity on the hypersphere.

Other of my interests include $p$-value combination methods and asymptotic theory.
